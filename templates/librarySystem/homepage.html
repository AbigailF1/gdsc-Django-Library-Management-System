Library Management System

This is a web-based system designed to manage the borrowing and returning of library materials. It streamlines the process for both librarians and students, making library resources more accessible and efficient.

Admin Student

About the Library Management System

This system allows students to browse the library catalog, search for specific materials, and reserve or borrow items. They can also track their borrowing history and receive notifications about due dates.

Library Rules

  • Students can borrow a maximum of 5 items at a time.
  • The loan period is typically 2 weeks, with the option to renew if no one else has placed a hold on the item.
  • Overdue fees will be applied to late returns.
  • Lost or damaged items must be reported to the library staff.
